SERVICES

Los Sistemas de Información Geográfica (SIG o GIS, en su acrónimo inglés) son herramientas transversales a una gran cantidad de disciplinas que tienen como objetivo fundamental capturar información geográfica, representarla y analizarla para una correcta toma de decisiones.

Compilation geographic information

We are able to collect data in the field with the most advanced means. From drones to the use of GPS, through laser scanner or LiDAR.

Geographic information representation

The representation of geographic information on the web, or web mapping, is revolutionizing the way of visualizing the data, placing value on geolocation as a way to understand complex phenomena that happen in the territory.

Geographical information analysis

Data Analysis using GIS (Geographic Information Systems) tools allows us to generate reports that provide added value to our clients in strategic decision making.

Precision farming

The use of drones which transported multispectral cameras, as well as other sensors, allow efficient control of cultivate, increasing production and saving costs.

Education

We like to share our knowledge with others. We offer tailor-made courses, both personal and online.
